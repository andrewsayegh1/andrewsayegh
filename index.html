My Methodology

"The system wasn't built with us in mind. But we can learn to navigate it—and change it from the inside."

I grew up in California and started at community college without a counselor who understood what "competitive applications" meant. I figured it out through relentless research and iteration.

That process took me to UCLA, internships on Capitol Hill, two national fellowships, and graduate admissions to Columbia School of International and Public Affairs. Today, I turn that lived experience into strategy for ambitious students and young professionals.
